Explore our portfolio of successful projects, showcasing our expertise in environmental monitoring, hydrographic surveying, and custom solution development.

Hydrographic Survey of Emirate of Dubai

A turnkey solution including a fully-equipped survey vessel, multibeam survey with 100% bottom coverage, and an integrated geospatial marine database for planning and decision making.

Bio-Digital Survey of Marine Habitats in the UAE

A Public-Private Partnership with the UAE Ministry of Climate Change and Environment (MOCCAE) to deliver machine learning and robotics to survey marine life along the Emirati coast.

Integrated Environmental Monitoring Vehicle

A compact, vehicle-based monitoring system for Dubai Municipality, integrating sensors for air pollutants, noise, electromagnetic radiation, and weather parameters with real-time tracking.

Odor Monitoring System for ENOC

An independent and automated cloud-based system for odor monitoring near the Expo 2020 site, with real-time data transmission to the Dubai Municipality's central monitoring database.
